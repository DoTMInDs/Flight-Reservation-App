Terms & Conditions

Reservation Only - Not a Ticket

This system creates flight reservations (PNR) only. No tickets are issued until payment is completed.

Hold Period

Reservations are held for 24 hours from creation. After this period, the reservation automatically cancels.

Fare Rules

Fares are subject to change until ticketed. Airlines may change prices without notice.

Cancellation

Un-ticketed reservations can be cancelled anytime at no charge. Ticketed bookings follow airline rules.

Data Privacy

Passenger information is encrypted and stored securely per GDPR requirements.
